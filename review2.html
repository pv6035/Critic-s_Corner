Fair Play (2023)

Directed by: Chloe Domont

Release Year: 2023

Runtime: 113 minutes

IMDB Rating: 7.0/10

Cast: Phoebe Dynevor, Alden Ehrenreich, Eddie Marsan, Rich Sommer

Rating: ★★☆☆☆

Fair Play is a gripping psychological thriller that delves into the dark side of ambition and the complexities of relationships in a high-stakes environment. Directed by Chloe Domont, the film tells the story of a young couple navigating the cutthroat world of finance and the consequences that arise when one of them gets a major promotion.

Phoebe Dynevor and Alden Ehrenreich deliver powerful performances, capturing the tension and emotional turmoil that comes with their characters' struggles. The film expertly balances moments of intimacy with suspenseful twists, making it a captivating watch from start to finish.

Domont's direction and the film's sharp writing create a tense atmosphere that keeps the audience engaged, reflecting the ruthless nature of the corporate world. Fair Play is a thought-provoking thriller that raises questions about power, trust, and the lengths people will go to achieve their ambitions.
