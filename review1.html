Extraction (2020)

Directed by: Sam Hargrave

Release Year: 2020

Runtime: 117 minutes

IMDB Rating: 6.7/10

Cast: Chris Hemsworth, Rudhraksh Jaiswal, Randeep Hooda, Golshifteh Farahani

Rating: ★★★★☆

Extraction is a thrilling action film that delivers high-octane sequences and intense performances. Directed by Sam Hargrave, this film follows Tyler Rake (played by Chris Hemsworth), a black ops mercenary tasked with rescuing the kidnapped son of an international crime lord.

The action choreography is nothing short of spectacular, showcasing some of the best stunt work and fight scenes in recent cinema. Hargrave, a former stunt coordinator, brings a visceral energy to the film that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats.

While the plot may not break new ground, the emotional depth brought by the characters adds a layer of complexity to the story. Hemsworth delivers a compelling performance, balancing brute strength with vulnerability. Extraction is a must-watch for action enthusiasts and showcases the best of modern action filmmaking.
