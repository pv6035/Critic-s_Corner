Anthropoid (2016)

Directed by: Sean Ellis

Release Year: 2016

Runtime: 120 minutes

IMDB Rating: 7.2/10

Cast: Cillian Murphy, Jamie Dornan, Charlotte Le Bon, Anna Geislerová, Harry Lloyd

Rating: ★★★★½

Anthropoid is a gripping and intense portrayal of the true story of Operation Anthropoid, the World War II mission to assassinate SS General Reinhard Heydrich. Directed by Sean Ellis, the film excels in capturing the tension and moral dilemmas faced by the operatives involved in this daring mission.

Cillian Murphy and Jamie Dornan deliver powerful performances as the lead characters, adding depth to the narrative with their portrayal of complex and conflicted individuals. The film’s cinematography and pacing effectively build suspense, making the final act particularly harrowing.

The historical accuracy, combined with the human element, creates a film that is both informative and emotionally resonant. Anthropoid is a commendable entry in the war film genre, offering a lesser-known but important story with skill and respect.
